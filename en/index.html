Our Story

Tenuta le Rogge was born from an act of love for the land of Maremma. Alberto and his family took on the fascinating challenge of bringing abandoned vineyards back to life on a hill just seven kilometers from the charming village of Scansano. Shortly after, Simone's enthusiasm joined the project, strengthening the vision. Together, we work with dedication to craft wines that are a faithful expression of the authenticity and unmistakable character of the splendid Tuscan Maremma. Every bottle is a story of rebirth, a tribute to the territory and the passion that guides us.

Our Philosophy

At Tenuta le Rogge, wine production is a symphony of respect and innovation. We firmly believe in a sustainable approach, where nature is not just a resource, but our main ally and inspiration. From the meticulous and respectful care of our vines, cultivated with attention to preserve their vitality and expression, to the aging in the cellar, every phase is orchestrated to enhance the unique terroir that hosts us. Our hills, kissed by the Maremma sun and caressed by the sea breeze, instill in our grapes that distinctive character that is then found in every sip.
